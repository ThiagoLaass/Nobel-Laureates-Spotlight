The first items of the museum were collected back in the 1900s, now being part of the museum.

First founded in 1909. Being the main museum in the country, made in honor to the folklorist Jakob hurt.

Tartu, city where the museum is located, is the second biggest city in Estonia.

Architects: Tsuyoshi Tane, Lina Ghotmeh, Dan Dorell, Tomomi Hayashi, Hanno Grossschmidt.

The main reason to build the museum was to make the history of Estonia stronger over time.

The age of the museum doesnt stop it from being very modern-looking and technological.

The interior is very beatiful and detaled, just like the view from outside.
